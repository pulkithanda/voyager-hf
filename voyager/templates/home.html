About Our Voyager


Since 2021,Voyager has established itself as a unique project. Serving the efforts of NASA and SpaceXtowards helping humanity's multi-planetary Presence. They are looking for 50 volunteers who would be aboardthe next starship to Mars-the first one for tourism. NASA needs volunteers from all over the world for this highly selective and historic mission, and thus they need a robust platform through which they can connect with the applicants.


This is where we come into the scene. "The Voyager" has been formed to complete this volunteer recruitment mission. We were formed as a part of the treaty of Space Poggers held between all the major countries and major space institutions dated 2 may 21.
